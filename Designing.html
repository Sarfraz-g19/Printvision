BANNER

A banner is a large graphic display used for advertising, promotion, or communication, typically featured in a prominent location such as at the top of a webpage, in a printed format, or as a hanging display at an event.

They are often used to promote special offers, new products, events, or brand awareness, and can be created in various sizes and formats depending on their purpose and placement.

POSTER

A poster is a large printed or digital visual display used to communicate information, promote events, or advertise products or services. Typically designed to be eye-catching and informative, posters combine images, graphics, and text to convey a message quickly and effectively.

Posters are commonly used for promoting concerts, movies, campaigns, sales, or upcoming events. Posters are versatile tools for communication and marketing, allowing for a combination of art and messaging to make a lasting impression.

STICKER

A sticker is a small, adhesive-backed graphic or design that can be applied to a variety of surfaces for decoration, identification, or promotional purposes. Stickers come in many shapes, sizes, and styles, often featuring images, logos, slogans, or artwork.

Stickers are versatile and cost-effective tools for promoting a business, event, or cause, and they can also serve as creative or fun accessories.

FLEX

Flex design emphasizes versatility, allowing elements to shift, resize, or reposition in response to user needs or spatial constraints. This principle is essential in areas like architecture, product design, and interior design.

For example, modular furniture is a form of flex design, as it can be rearranged to fit different purposes or environments.

BUSINESS CARD

A business card is a small, printed card that provides key information about an individual or company, typically including the person's name, job title, company name, and contact details such as phone number, email, and physical address.

They are widely used in professional settings, at meetings, events, and conferences, to make connections and leave a lasting impression.

INVITATION CARD

An invitation card is a printed or digital card used to formally invite someone to an event, celebration, or gathering, such as a wedding, birthday, corporate event, or party.

Invitation cards are often designed to match the theme or tone of the event, using colors, fonts, and images that reflect the occasion's mood—whether elegant, festive, or professional.
